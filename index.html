About the GAN United Project

StyleGANv2

StyleGAN is a generative adversarial network created by Nvidia researchers in 2018. It is arguably one of the best performing GANs of all time. StyleGANv2 uses a technology known as Differential Augmentation. This is the idea that individual images can be adjusted to keep the model's perspective diverse. This leads to a significantly higher performing network.

DCGAN

DCGAN uses advanced convolutional neural network architectures. Our original model was a DCGAN and created images of dogs. However, it ended up being really slow resulting in our switch to a StyleGANv2 architecture.

How GANs work

A GAN which stands for Generative Adversarial Network, was developed in 2014 by Ian Goodfellow. GAN's work by using a Generator and a Discriminator that contest with each other like they are playing a game. A Generator creates the images, while the discriminator tells if the image is real or fake. Based of if the Generator is found it, it will improve and create better looking images. Similarly, the Discriminator, will also improve if it is continually getting images wrong making its identification process more accurate.
